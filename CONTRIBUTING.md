# Contributing to Professional Journey Archive

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this repository!

## Guidelines

### Adding Company Information

1. **Company Documentation** should include:
   - Company overview and history during the tenure period
   - Technology focus and transformation
   - Role and responsibilities
   - Professional development activities
   - Certifications and training completed

2. **Structure your additions:**
   ```markdown
   # Company Name
   
   ## Company Overview
   [Description and history]
   
   ## Technology Focus
   [Technology platforms and evolution]
   
   ## Professional Development
   [Courses, certifications, activities]
   ```

### Adding Certifications and Courses

1. **Create documentation** with dates, issuing organizations, and relevance to professional development
2. **Update timeline** to reflect new entries
3. **Organize chronologically** within documents

### Adding Images

- Place all images in the `assets/` folder
- Use descriptive filenames (e.g., `atos-office-rome.jpg`)
- Supported formats: PNG, JPG, GIF, SVG
- Keep image sizes reasonable (< 2MB when possible)

### Commit Messages

Use clear, descriptive commit messages:
- `Add: Company information - description`
- `Update: Certifications - new entries`
- `Fix: Document correction`

## Code of Conduct

- Be respectful and professional
- Focus on content accuracy
- Maintain the archival and reflective tone
- Credit original sources and organizations

## Questions?

For questions or suggestions, please open an issue or contact the repository maintainer.
